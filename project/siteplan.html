Purpose

Provide users with a platform to buy organic groceries online with home delivery.

Target Audience & Scenarios

Primary audience: Health-conscious individuals and families looking for organic products.

Scenario 1: How do I find the best organic products for my dietary preferences?

Answer 1: Users can filter products by categories like vegetables, fruits, cereales, etc., helping them find what aligns with their dietary needs quickly.

Scenario 2: How can I get the products delivered to my home?

Answer 2: After adding items to the cart, users can easily proceed to checkout where they will input their shipping address and payment method. Delivery options will be displayed based on their location.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #6DBE45 (Fresh Green) - Used for header, call-to-action buttons.
  • Secondary Color: #2C6F2A (Dark Green) - Used for footer, navigation menu.

Typography

Font: Roboto

  • Body text: Roboto Regular, size 16px
  • Headings: Roboto Bold, size 24px
  • Buttons: Roboto Medium, size 18px

    • These fonts were selected for their clean, modern look, which enhances the user experience by improving readability on both desktop and mobile devices.
